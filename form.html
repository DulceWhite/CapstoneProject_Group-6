Tell your story

You can help bring awareness to flooding problems in your community.

At Flood Talk we believe that floods are a big problem that needs to be listen to. Floods are a force of nature that can cause hazardous circumstances that could put human life at risk and generate material loses. When you share a testimony you help people understand the severity, the suffering, the struggle. We want to listen to you and help turn heads in this direction to promote solutions.

If you know somebody has been a victim to flood, please encourage them to share their story in the form below so the areas of high risk can be identified and address.
